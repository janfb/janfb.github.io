Leading the technical development of an open-source ML pipeline that turns a 2024 hackathon prototype into a production-ready QGIS plugin for environmental monitoring of *Streuobstwiesen* (traditional orchards). The system fine-tunes SAM (Segment Anything Model) with LoRA adapters on satellite imagery, with MLFlow-based experiment tracking and a Gradio web frontend for non-technical users.
