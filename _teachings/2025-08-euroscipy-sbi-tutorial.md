A 1.5-hour intermediate-level tutorial walking through the end-to-end SBI workflow: defining parameter ranges and a-priori checks, running NPE/NLE/NRE-style methods, a-posteriori validation, and interpreting results. Examples drawn from epidemiology, neuroscience, particle physics, and engineering.
